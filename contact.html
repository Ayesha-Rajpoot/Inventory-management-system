Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with InventorHub?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free account, set up your products, and start managing your inventory. Our onboarding team will guide you through the process.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to get started.

Can I integrate InventorHub with my existing systems?

Absolutely! We have APIs and integrations with popular platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, Stripe, and more.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer 24/7 email support, live chat, and comprehensive documentation. Premium plans include phone support and dedicated account managers.

How is my data secured?

We use bank-level encryption (SSL/TLS), regular security audits, and comply with GDPR and other data protection regulations.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges accordingly.
